Screenshots Tour

Free Sign Up

top ^
Go to CSI Platform and complete all the relevant fields in the registration form. Click on “FREE SIGN UP”.

Remember that Passwords must be at least 8 characters long including numbers, upper and lower case letters.

Go to the inbox of the email used during registration and check for an email with the instructions on how to activate your account.

After following the instruction you will be ready to log into the CSI Platform.		 "
top ^
Log in at CSI Platform using your email and password (the same used during registration) and click on “LOG IN”.

You will be now logged in the tool on the main “Dashboard” page. The dashboard shows the summary of all your site emissions.		 "

Add Construction Sites

top ^
From Dashboard go and click on “Add Site”.

You will then be directed to the “Add Site” page.		 "
top ^
Fill out all the fields that have an asterisk.

Don´t forget to include Site´s location.

Once all fields are completed click on “Save”.		 "
top ^
You have just added a new Site to the CSI tool and redirected to the “Site” page.

You are now ready to add “Meters” to the tool to measure, monitor, set targets and report on your site carbon emissions.
 "

Add Meters

top ^
The CSI platform allow you to add as many meter as you like and cover many aspects of your site impacts such as Electricity use, Fuel consumption, Transport, Waste and Water.

From “Site” page go and click on “Meters”. You will then be directed to the “Meters” page where you can see all the meters used on site.		 "
top ^
The tool defaults to the electricity meter.

Click on “+Add Meter” on the right hand side of the screen. You are then taken to the add electricity meter page.		 "
top ^
Fill out all the fields and once completed click on “Save”.

You have now added a new electricity meter to the CSI tool and you will be redirected to the new “Electricity Meter” page.		 "

Upload Data

top ^
CSI platform sign up is free but you need to have a subscription to upload your data and get all functionalities.
We charge a flat fee for your subscription based on the project construction cost.

You will be ready to load values into your meters after subscribe.
Check Our Prices

Now to input meter readings click in the LOAD VALUES button on the left hand side menu.

The tool defaults to the previous meter reading. You just have to input the date when the reading is taken and hit “Enter reading values”.		 "
top ^
The screen will reload and a new field will appear for you to enter the actual meter reading.

Enter the value in the blank box and hit the “Save” button.		 "
top ^
You were redirected to the “SERIES” page to view your first meter readings and correspondent emissions.

If you make a mistake click in SERIES button and the tool will take you to the page with the loaded values. You can either edit or delete the value.		 "
